# PassMan 🔐

PassMan is a secure, lightweight, and user-friendly password manager designed to help you store, generate, and manage your credentials safely. Stop reusing passwords or writing them down on sticky notes—PassMan keeps your digital life organized and secure.

## 🚀 Features

*   **Secure Storage:** Encrypts and safely stores your account credentials.
*   **Password Generator:** Built-in tool to generate strong, customizable, and random passwords.
*   **Search & Organize:** Easily search through your saved credentials.
*   **User-Friendly Interface:** Simple and intuitive design for effortless password management.
